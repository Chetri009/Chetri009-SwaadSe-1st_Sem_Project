Perfect Grilled Fish with Herbs and Lemon

A flavorful, tender grilled fish marinated with herbs, garlic, and zesty lemon.

Ingredients:

  • 2 Whole Fish (such as sea bass or snapper), cleaned and scaled
  • 2 Lemons (1 sliced, 1 for juicing)
  • 3 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 3 Garlic Cloves, minced
  • Fresh Herbs (such as parsley, dill, or thyme)
  • Salt and Black Pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp Paprika (optional)

Steps:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat and lightly oil the grates.
  2. Rinse the fish and pat dry with a paper towel. Make 3-4 slits on each side of the fish.
  3. In a small bowl, mix olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, paprika (if using), salt, and pepper.
  4. Rub the mixture all over the fish, including inside the slits and cavity.
  5. Stuff the cavity with lemon slices and fresh herbs.
  6. Place the fish on the grill and cook for 4-5 minutes per side, or until the fish is flaky and cooked through.
  7. Carefully remove the fish from the grill and drizzle with additional lemon juice before serving.
  8. Garnish with extra herbs and serve with a side of grilled vegetables or salad.
