Chocolate Berry Mousse Cake

A decadent dessert with layers of moist chocolate cake, silky chocolate mousse.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup All-Purpose Flour
  • 1/2 cup Cocoa Powder
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1/2 cup Unsalted Butter (softened)
  • 3/4 cup Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1/2 cup Milk

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F) and grease a round cake tin.
  2. In a bowl, sift together flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and baking soda.
  3. In another bowl, cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Add eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition. Stir in vanilla extract.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, alternating with milk. Mix until smooth.
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared cake tin and smooth the top.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  8. Let the cake cool in the tin for 10 minutes, then transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely.
